When properly supervised, TRT is safe and well-tolerated for most men with clinically low testosterone. Common mild side effects include injection site reactions and slight changes in hematocrit (red blood cell count), which are monitored through regular bloodwork. Our physicians tailor dosing to minimize risk and adjust protocols as needed. TRT is not appropriate for men trying to conceive — we discuss this during your consultation.
